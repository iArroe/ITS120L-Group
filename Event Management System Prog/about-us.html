Welcome to Laugh & Draft Resto Bar!

When it comes to having a good time and filling your belly, we’ve got all the punchlines you need! At Laugh & Draft, we believe that a hearty laugh is the best side dish to your favorite meal. Join us as we serve up not just delicious food and drinks but also a side of comedy that will have you rolling in the aisles!

So, what exactly is Laugh & Draft all about? Picture this: you’re munching on our mouthwatering nachos while a comedian on stage delivers jokes so funny, they should come with a warning label! Just like our menu adapts to the season, our comedy acts change every night, tailored to tickle your funny bone and suit the vibe. Whether it’s stand-up, improv, or a hilarious open mic night, we promise the only thing you’ll regret is not coming sooner!

The Laugh Conference

When I first jumped into the comedy bar scene, I was on the hunt for the perfect formula that would pack the house, turn me into the comedy king of the city, and have people lining up for laughs every night! Now, after being in the business for a while, I can look back on my early dreams with a chuckle. Turns out, it’s not just about the punchlines but creating a space where everyone leaves with sore cheeks from laughing too hard.

Our Story

2018

The Birth of Laugh & Draft Resto Bar

What started as a dream to combine great food with great laughs became a reality! With a cozy atmosphere and a menu full of delicious bites, we opened our doors to the public, inviting everyone to unwind and enjoy a good time.

2019

The First Comedy Night

Our first open mic night was a hit! Local comedians took the stage, and laughter echoed through the bar. It was clear: the combination of tasty drafts and hilarious acts was a recipe for success.

2021

Bringing the Funny with Themed Events

There is no better advertisement campaign that is low cost and also successful at the same time. Great business ideas when utilized effectively can save lots of money.

2023

Celebrating a Community of Laughter

As we continued to grow, we established partnerships with local comedians and hosted charity events, using laughter as a way to give back. Our bar became a beloved gathering spot for those seeking joy and community spirit.

2024

Cheers to Laughter and Great Times!

With our loyal patrons and a steady lineup of comedians, we’re committed to being the go-to destination for laughter and delicious food. Join us for an unforgettable night of comedy, drinks, and the best company around!
